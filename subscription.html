Subscription Agreement

1. Subscription Terms

By subscribing to the Kenwood Water Purifier Subscription Plan ("the Service"), you ("the Subscriber") agree to be bound by the following terms and conditions. This agreement is entered into between you and Elken Marketing (M) Sdn Bhd ("the Company").

2. Subscription Plans

The Service offers the following plans:

  • Monthly Plan: MYR 128 per month, billed monthly. Subscriber may cancel at any time with 30 days prior written notice.
  • Annual Plan (12 Months): MYR 98 per month, billed annually at MYR 1,176. First month promotional rate of MYR 68. This plan is a 12-month commitment and is non-cancellable during the commitment period.

3. Service Coverage

The subscription includes the following services at no additional cost:

  • Professional installation of the Kenwood Water Purifier at the Subscriber's registered address
  • Replacement of water filters every 6 months
  • Full maintenance and repair coverage for the duration of the subscription
  • Eligibility for a free model upgrade after 24 continuous months of subscription

Payment shall be made via the payment method selected at checkout. For monthly plans, payment is due on the same date each month. Installation will be scheduled within 5-7 business days of the first payment. The Subscriber must ensure that a suitable water source and electrical outlet are available at the installation location. The Company reserves the right to reschedule installation due to unforeseen circumstances.

The water purifier unit remains the property of the Company throughout the subscription period. Upon termination or expiry of the subscription, the Subscriber must return the equipment in good working condition, subject to normal wear and tear.

Annual Plan: The 12-month commitment is non-cancellable. Early termination will incur a penalty fee equal to the remaining months of the commitment period multiplied by MYR 50. After the initial 12-month period, the subscription will automatically renew on a monthly basis unless cancelled.
